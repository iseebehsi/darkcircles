How to Reduce Dark Circles with Laser Treatment

Dark circles are circles of black or dark browns that can occur anywhere on the face. They can be created by a variety of factors, including poor diet, stress, and/or sunlight exposure. Laser treatment can help to reduce these circles by destroying the melanin in the skin, which will lighten it up and make the area appear more youthful and radiant.
